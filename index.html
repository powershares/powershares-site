Japan Property Research

Photo, Video, and Drone Filming Available

We provide on-site property research in Japan for overseas clients, including local area review, still images, video coverage, and optional drone filming.

We can cover nearby public transportation access, the route and environment around the property, surrounding commercial facilities, and the building exterior. Our main service area is Kanagawa and Tokyo. Other areas may require additional fees. Orders from within Japan are also accepted.
